Emergency Preparedness System

Purpose
  • •To help businesses and communities ensure emergency preparedness
  • •Emergency preparedness being broadly defined to include preparedness for natural and man-made hazards as well for threats to business continuity resulting from these hazards or from other, i.e., non-life threatening,  events.
Key Features
  • •Provides a consistent framework for undertaking planning, testing and other activities related to disaster response and/or business continuity
  • •Pre-loaded with data for rapid plan development.  Data based on recommendations of major disaster relief organizations and experience with business continuity.
